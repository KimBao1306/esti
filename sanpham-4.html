nguyên lý hoạt động

  • Sample is dried by heating and burnt (pyrolysis) in oxygen or air. Its decomposed product is oxidized by the high temperature catalyst carried via oxygen.
  • Moisture is exhausted by pneumatic truncation, and the rest of decomposed product is further conveyed to sub-nanometer Hg-trap.
  • Meanwhile, interferent like halogen, nitric oxides, and sulfur oxides are absorbed by catalyst.
  • Mercury atoms are selectively captured, and experience secondary atomization by heating Hgtrap
